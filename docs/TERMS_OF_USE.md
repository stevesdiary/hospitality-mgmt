## 6. Booking Process and Confirmation

### 6.1 Making a Reservation

To make a Reservation, Guests must:

1. Search and select a Hotel and room;
2. Choose check-in/check-out dates and number of guests;
3. Complete the payment process;
4. Receive a **Booking Confirmation** containing a unique booking ID, a QR code,
   and booking details.

A Reservation becomes binding when the Guest receives a Booking Confirmation
email and the Platform records the booking as `confirmed`.

### 6.2 Booking Confirmation and QR Pass

Upon confirmed payment, the Guest receives a digital **QR-coded Booking Pass**
that contains their pre-filled details (name, email, room, dates, guest count).

present this QR code or their booking reference at the Hotel's front desk.

### 6.3 Booking Status Lifecycle

Reservations progress through the following statuses:

`pending` → `confirmed` → `checked-in` → `checked-out`

A booking may also be marked `cancelled` or `no-show` as described in Section 8.
